# Yearly: Reflection Framework

>  Assets year performace.

## Operation

> Assess your personal operational performance in last year's.

- What were your top 3-5 accomplishments this year?
- What were the biggest challenges I faced this year?

## Audit & Control
>  Conduct an audit of your personal operational performance in relation to last week's plan and its alignment with policy.

Questions:
- How well did my original plan for the year hold up?
- Was my plan realistic in terms of time and resource management?

## Intelligence
> [Fedback Loop] Gather, analyze, and interpret information that informs decision-making and strategic direction changes.

Questions:
- What small adjustments could I make to increase overall productivity and well-being?
- Are there emerging opportunities or challenges in my environment that I need to adapt to?
- What new skills or knowledge should I develop to better align with my personal growth and long-term plans?

## Coordination
> Reflect on the coordination of resources and collaboration with other actors to achieve weekly goals.

Questions:
- Did I successfully prioritize and balance competing demands on my time, energy, and attention?
- How can I better allocate my resources (time, energy, finances) to meet my long-term needs and goals?

## Policy
> This reflection focuses on evaluating the alignment between current policies and operations, considering necessary adjustments to long-term goals and potential policy changes.

Questions:
- How is the state of Bremontix?
- Does the long-term vision I am pursuing reflect who I truly want to become, or do I need to reconsider my direction?
