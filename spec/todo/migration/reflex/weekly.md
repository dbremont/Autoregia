# Weekly:  Reflection Framework 

## Operation
> Assess your **personal operational performance** in last week's plan.

Questions:
- What were the **major accomplishments** of this week?
- What were the **biggest challenges** I faced this week?
- Were there any tasks that I **consistently avoided**? What was the reason behind that?
- What **tasks took more time** than expected, and what could have been done differently?
- How was my **energy level** throughout the week? Did I take care of my physical and mental well-being?

## Audit & Control
> Conduct an audit of your **personal operational performance** in relation to last week's plan and its alignment with policy.

Questions:
- How well did my original **plan for the week hold up**?
- Was my plan realistic in terms of time and resource management?

## Intelligence
>  [Fedback Loop]  Gather, analyze, and interpret information that informs decision-making and strategic direction changes.

Questions:
- Is `Bremontix` resiliance?
- What **lessons did I learn** from setbacks or obstacles?
- Are there **emerging opportunities** or **challenges** in my **environment** that I need to adapt to?
- What **new skills** or **knowledge** should I develop to better align with my personal growth and long-term plans?
- What small adjustments could I make to increase overall productivity and well-being?
