## Domain

| Domain                     | What is changing?                        | Typical Sources                                  |
| -------------------------- | ---------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------ |
| **Politics & Governance**  | decisions, elections, regulation         | governments, legislatures, political journalists |
| **Economy & Markets**      | production, inflation, employment, trade | FRED, IMF, World Bank, Bloomberg, central banks  |
| **Science**                | new knowledge                            | arXiv, PubMed, Nature, SSRN                      |
| **Technology**             | new capabilities                         | GitHub, Hacker News, company blogs, patents      |
| **Business & Industry**    | firms, products, mergers                 | SEC filings, earnings calls, Crunchbase          |
| **Finance & Capital**      | investment, markets                      | SEC, exchanges, macro indicators                 |
| **Security & Geopolitics** | wars, diplomacy, cyber                   | ISW, CSIS, Reuters                               |
| **Environment**            | climate, disasters, resources            | NOAA, NASA, Copernicus                           |
| **Health**                 | disease, medicine                        | WHO, CDC, journals                               |
| **Culture & Society**      | values, demographics, education          | surveys, media, social networks                  |
| **Infrastructure**         | energy, transportation, internet         | operators, government statistics                 |
| **Law**                    | court decisions, regulations             | courts, legal databases                          |
