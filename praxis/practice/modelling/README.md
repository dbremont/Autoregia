# Datomatica

1. M5 Forecasting Competition: Hosted by the International Institute of Forecasters (IIF), this competition focuses on forecasting future sales at Walmart. Participants are challenged to predict the sales of 42,000+ time series products.
2. Kaggle Competitions: Kaggle regularly hosts time series forecasting competitions on various topics, including stock price prediction, demand forecasting, energy consumption prediction, and more. These competitions attract participants worldwide and offer real-world datasets and challenges.
3. Global Energy Forecasting Competition (GEFCom): Organized by the International Institute of Forecasters (IIF) and the IEEE Power & Energy Society (PES), GEFCom focuses on forecasting electricity demand and prices at different geographical levels.
4. The M4 Competition: A large-scale forecasting competition organized by the International Institute of Forecasters (IIF) to improve upon the M3-Competition by evaluating forecasting methods on a wide range of time series data.
5. Amazon Forecasting Challenge: Amazon periodically hosts forecasting challenges, focusing on predicting demand for various products sold on its platform. These challenges provide participants with real-world retail data.
6. Data Science Bowl: While not exclusively focused on time series data, the Data Science Bowl often includes challenges related to time series analysis and forecasting, such as predicting ocean health or medical diagnosis trends over time.
