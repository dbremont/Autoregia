# Derivative Operator

* What is the formal definition of the derivative operator for one real variable? What does the derivative represents?
* What is the derivative of $x^n$?
* What is the derivative of a constant $c$? What is the derivative of $7$?
* What is the derivative of $f(x) \pm g(x)$? What is the derivative of $x^3 + \sin(x)$?
* What is the derivative of $f(x) \cdot g(x)$? What is the derivative of $x^2 \cdot e^x$?
* What is the derivative of $\frac{f(x)}{g(x)}$? What is the derivative of $\frac{\sin(x)}{x}$?
* What is the derivative of $f(g(x))$? What is the derivative of $\sin(x^2)$?
* What is the derivative of $a^{f(x)}$? What is the derivative of $2^{x^3}$?
* What is the derivative of $\ln(f(x))$ or $\log_a(f(x))$? What is the derivative of $\ln(x^2 + 1)$?
* What is the derivative of $\sin(f(x))$, $\cos(f(x))$, or $\tan(f(x))$? What is the derivative of $\tan(3x)$?
* What is the derivative of $\arcsin(f(x))$, $\arctan(f(x))$, or other inverse trig functions? What is the derivative of $\arctan(2x)$?
* What is the derivative of $\sinh(f(x))$, $\cosh(f(x))$, or $\tanh(f(x))$? What is the derivative of $\sinh(x^2)$?
* What is the derivative of an implicit function $F(x,y)=0$? What is the derivative of $x^2 + y^2 - 1 = 0$?
* What is the derivative of parametric functions $x=f(t), y=g(t)$? What is the derivative of $x=t^2, y=t^3$?
* What is the derivative of $[f(x)]^{g(x)}$? What is the derivative of $(x^2 + 1)^{\sin(x)}$?
