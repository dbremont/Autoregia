## Minimal Physical Ontology

> Let’s develop  a minimal classical mechanics  ontology.
> 

| Category | Instance(s) | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Entity** | Particle, Rigid body, Object, Mass point, Continuous Media, Ensembles | Fundamental “things” that move or interact. |
| **Process** | Motion, Impulse, Work, Energy conservation, Thermal conduction | Processes describing changes in entities’ state or energy. |
| **System** | Multi-body system, Rigid body system, Continuous system, Engine system, Flow system | Collections of entities studied as a unit. |
| **Particle** | Point mass, Particle in motion | Simplified entities for modeling dynamics. |
| **Field** | Electromagnetic field, Gravitational field, Force field (Newtonian) | Continuous quantities mediating interactions. |
| **Force (Interaction)** | Gravity, Electromagnetic force, Contact force, Drag force, Centripetal force | Interactions causing acceleration or change in motion. |
| **Event** | Collision, Impulse application, Momentum exchange, Heat transfer | Occurrences affecting system states. |
| **Energy** | Kinetic energy, Potential energy, Thermal energy, Work | Quantities describing the ability to perform motion or change. |
| **Space** | 3D Euclidean space, Constrained space (circular motion, rolling) | Geometrical setting for motion. |
| **Observable** | Position, Momentum, Velocity, Acceleration, Angular momentum, Temperature | Measurable quantities characterizing motion and thermal state. |
| **Property** | Mass, Charge, Velocity, Angular velocity, Moment of inertia | Attributes of entities relevant to dynamics and EM effects. |
